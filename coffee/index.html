Pour-Over...

Is a beautifully simple and accessible way to brew a single cup of coffee with clean, fully developed flavor and body.

  1. Measure and boil
  2. Pre-moisten filter
  3. Measure and grind
  4. Pour and pause
  5. Complete the pour and enjoy

To make your pour-over more consistent, use a timer. It should take about 3 minutes to make a cup.
